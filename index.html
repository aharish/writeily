Writeily

A forward-thinking markdown editor for Android

Easy Writing

Writeily is an Android app that lets you take down notes in plain text, or Markdown format. Organize notes by starring the most important, and preview your work in a rendered Markdown view.

Forward-thinking Design

Inspired by the latest "Project Hera" Android leaks, the app blossoms in colour and minimalism. Switch between dark and light themes, and choose between your favourite Roboto font style.
